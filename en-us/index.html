About More

Minings Studios was created by a developer who loved HTML, but one day he had an idea:
- Why not create my own studio?
There was a lot of ambition there;
many developers said it was almost impossible to create an entire game in HTML, but it's "almost," not impossible.

That's when a table emerged:
Diamonds and iron are the main resources (economy and design),
redstone is used to power the code, and finally, netherite: It's your contribution to the project;
it's not about paying, just entering the game and enjoying it is already a great contribution.

Mining Studios strives. Come and try it!
